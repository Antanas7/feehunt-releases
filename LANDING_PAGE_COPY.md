## Problem Section

Subscriptions are easy to start and easy to forget. Payment warnings hide between newsletters, promotions, receipts, and routine notifications. Over time, your inbox becomes harder to understand and your money becomes harder to track.

FeeHunt gives you a practical way to review what matters, reduce email noise, and stay ahead of unwanted charges.

## Features

### Subscription Detection

FeeHunt scans recent Gmail messages to help identify recurring payments, subscription notices, renewals, invoices, and trial-related emails.

### Financial Risk Alerts

FeeHunt highlights emails that may need urgent attention, including failed payments, declined cards, overdue invoices, suspended accounts, and billing problems.

### One-Click Cancel Subscription

When possible, FeeHunt helps you open the best available unsubscribe, billing, account, or cancellation page so you can take action faster.

### Inbox Cleanup

Review promotional emails, newsletters, and shop messages. Archive, delete, mark important, or manage senders directly from FeeHunt.

### Security Shield (Coming Soon)

Future FeeHunt versions will help detect phishing and scam patterns so users can better protect their inboxes and accounts.

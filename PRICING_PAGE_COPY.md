## Billing FAQ

### Is there a free trial?

Yes. Both Basic and Family plans include a 7-day free trial.

### When will I be charged?

After the 7-day trial ends, your selected plan becomes active unless you cancel before the trial ends.

### Can I cancel anytime?

Yes. FeeHunt will provide a clear cancel subscription button before public billing enforcement is enabled.

### Are there hidden fees?

No. FeeHunt uses simple monthly pricing with no hidden fees.

### Can I change plans later?

Yes. Plan upgrades and billing self-service are planned for the public launch customer portal.

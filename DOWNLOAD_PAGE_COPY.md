# Download Page Copy

## Installation Steps

1. Download the FeeHunt beta ZIP file.
2. Extract the ZIP file to a folder on your computer.
3. Open `FeeHunt.exe`.
4. FeeHunt starts a local app in your browser at `http://localhost:8501`.
5. Click `Connect Gmail`.
6. Sign in to Google.

## Gmail Sign-In

FeeHunt connects to Gmail using Google's OAuth authorization system. You approve
access through Google instead of giving FeeHunt your password.

No manual `credentials.json` setup is required in FeeHunt Beta v1.1. FeeHunt
opens Google's sign-in window and saves your token locally on your computer.

## Local Data

FeeHunt stores tokens, settings, and scan results locally in your Windows user
profile. Gmail cleanup actions such as archive, delete, mark as spam, and mark
as important affect your real Gmail account. Use them thoughtfully.
